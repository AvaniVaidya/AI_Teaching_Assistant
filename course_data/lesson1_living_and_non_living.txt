Lesson 1: Living and Non-Living Things

Introduction:
Everything around us can be classified as living or non-living.

Characteristics of Living Things:
- They grow
- They breathe
- They need food
- They reproduce

Non-Living Things:
Non-living things do not show life processes.

Examples:
Living: humans, animals, plants  
Non-living: table, stone, book

Summary:
Living things show life processes, non-living things do not.
